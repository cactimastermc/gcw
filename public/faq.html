FAQ

How do I redeem a Minecraft Cape/Cosmetic?

  1. Go to minecraft.net/redeem.
  2. Log in with your Mojang/Microsoft account.
  3. Enter the code or claim link from your purchase.
  4. Open Minecraft Bedrock Edition and go to your profile settings.
  5. Equip the cape or cosmetic from the cosmetics menu.

    6. ---- Java Edition Only ----

  6. On the Minecraft Launcher or on the website go to Skin Settings and equip the cape (no bedrock cosmetics) to get it on Java Edition!
